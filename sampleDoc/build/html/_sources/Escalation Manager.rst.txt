Escalation Manager 
============================

Responsability
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

	1. The Escalation Manager and On-Call Engineer shifts run from Monday 1200MT to Monday 1159MT 

	2. The Escalation Manager must contact the On-Call Engineer and ensure they know they are on call and that VictorOps is set appropriately and expectations are understood. 

	3. Non-DU Call Outs 

		a. Under rare circumstances support may request after hours help for a non DU case. It is the Sustaining Engineering Manager's call if they wish to allow this to happpen.
